The Data Behind
Global Wealth

This application visualizes data from the UBS Global Wealth Report, providing an interactive exploration of wealth distribution across the world.

Data Source

The data covers the period from 2000 to 2022 and includes wealth estimates by region and country, high-net-worth individual statistics, components of wealth, and distribution patterns.

Methodology

Wealth is defined as the value of financial assets plus real assets (principally housing) owned by households, minus their debts. The report provides estimates for adults (persons aged 20 and over) in over 200 countries.
